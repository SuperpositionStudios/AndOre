Fence
=======
* Icon: `#`
* Build with: `b, 1, *`
* Build Cost: `30 Ore`
* Ore Dropped on Destruction: `10 Ore`
* Health: '30'

#### Uses

Fences are a low cost building that can be used to create a low health wall. Players will often use fences to mark the edge of their territory.
