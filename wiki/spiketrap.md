Spike Trap
=======
* Icon: `S`
* Build with: `b, 8, *`
* Build Cost: `150 Ore`
* Ore Dropped on Destruction: `50 Ore`
* Health: `40`
* Damage: `5`

#### Uses
Damages enemy(E) and neutral(N) players that move onto it.
Will not damage the builder or anyone in the Corp of the builder.
